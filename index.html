Greece

Though it may not be the first vacation destination to come to mind, Greece has slowly become one of the most popular tourist and vacationing spots in the world, often ranking within the top 20 countries to visit. Greece is home to Athens, Crete, and hundreds of islands along with ancient structures that give glimpses into the lives of past societies. In 2011, it was recorded that Greece had a record of 16.5 million foreign visitors in the country.
